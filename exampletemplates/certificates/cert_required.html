What are client certificates?

Client certificates are digital certificates stored in your browser that prove your identity and provide secure access to protected systems.

How to get a certificate?

Contact your system administrator to request a client certificate. They will provide you with a P12 file and installation instructions.

Installation Steps:
  1. Obtain your P12 certificate file from your administrator
  2. Import the certificate into your browser's certificate store
  3. Restart your browser
  4. Return to this page and select your certificate when prompted
